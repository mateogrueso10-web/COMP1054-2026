Viva la Vida

By Coldplay

About the Song

"Viva la Vida" explores themes of power, loss, and reflection. The lyrics are written from the perspective of a fallen king looking back on his past glory and current downfall. The song became one of Coldplay's biggest hits and is known for its orchestral sound.

About the Band

Coldplay is a British rock band formed in 1997 in London. The band consists of Chris Martin (vocals, piano), Jonny Buckland (guitar, backing vocals), Guy Berryman (bass, backing vocals), and Will Champion (drums, percussion).
