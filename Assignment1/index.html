Viva la Vida

By Coldplay

About the Song

"Viva la Vida" explores themes of power, loss, and reflection. The lyrics are written from the perspective of a fallen king looking back on his past glory and current downfall. The song became one of Coldplay's biggest hits and is known for its orchestral sound.
